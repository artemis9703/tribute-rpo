Gameplay Rules

While the OASIS is a near-perfect community, there are still rules we all have to abide by.

  • No Real-World Identity Disclosure
  • No Cheating or Exploits
  • No Unauthorized Access
  • No Unauthorized Item or Coin Transfers
  • No Fighting in Safe Zones
  • No Blackmail or Real-World Harm by the OASIS
  • No Permanent Item Hoarding by Alternate Accounts
  • No Unrestricted Teleportation
  • No Altering Core World Rules
